FarmTech Hub – Site Plan

Site Name

FarmTech Hub – A platform designed to help farmers explore modern agricultural solutions, learn about sustainable practices, and connect with agri-tech resources in Kenya and beyond.

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to educate and inspire farmers by showcasing smart farming technologies, digital tools, and success stories that enhance productivity and sustainability.

Scenarios

  • How can technology help me manage my farm more efficiently?
  • Where can I find reliable farming tools and export opportunities?
  • What are the latest innovations in irrigation, soil management, and pest control?

Color Schema

The site uses the same color palette as the temple/farm project:

  • Primary Color – #055a05 (Deep Green)
  • Secondary Color – #f4a261 (Warm Orange)
  • Accent 1 – #264653 (Dark Blue-Green)
  • Accent 2 – #e9c46a (Golden Sand)

Typography

Headings: Merriweather, serif

Body Text: Lato, sans-serif
